Frequently Asked Questions

  • What do I need to get started?

    We will need the following to get started: Brand Name, Tagline (optional), Website (optional), Business Description, Preferred Logo Style (optional), Color Preference (optional), Notes/Requirements, Own idea/suggestion you can have (Optional).

  • Why do I need vector or source file?

    The most important reason to have a font or vector file is so that you have the ability to edit and manipulate your own logo. It provides a level of ownership of your own product that you would not otherwise have.

  • What does social media kit include?

    Profile picture and banner/cover design for your social medias e.g. Facebook, Twitter, etc.

  • What is Stationary Designs?

    Stationery designs are the set of business cards and letterheads that are designed symmetrically to the overall theme and colors of your logo design. It comes in direct print ready files in RGB/CMYK depending on your needs.
